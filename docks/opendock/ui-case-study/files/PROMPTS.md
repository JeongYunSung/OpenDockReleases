# UI Case Study Prompts

## Review Case Study

Review this case study for clarity, credibility, narrative flow, role clarity,
and evidence quality. Identify missing proof and vague claims.

## Strengthen Problem

Improve this problem statement. Make the affected user, pain, context, and
constraint specific.

## Measure Impact

Suggest credible impact metrics and qualitative evidence for this case study.
Separate measurable outcomes from inferred outcomes.
