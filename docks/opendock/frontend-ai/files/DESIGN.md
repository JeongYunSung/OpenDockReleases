# Frontend Design Notes

## UI Principles

- Prioritize clarity over decoration.
- Keep spacing, typography, and interaction states consistent.
- Ensure text fits at mobile and desktop widths.
- Prefer familiar controls and accessible labels.

## Required States

- Loading
- Empty
- Error
- Disabled
- Success
