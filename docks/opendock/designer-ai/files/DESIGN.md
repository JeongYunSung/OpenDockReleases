## Interface Principles

- Make primary actions obvious.
- Keep information hierarchy scannable.
- Use spacing and typography consistently.
- Meet WCAG AA contrast for body text and controls.
- Design mobile and desktop states together.

## Review Checklist

- Can a new user understand the next action within five seconds?
- Are empty, loading, error, and success states covered?
- Does the layout still work at narrow widths?
- Are labels specific enough for non-expert users?
